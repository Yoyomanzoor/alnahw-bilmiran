\begin{english}
    \boldred{\textarabic{المُركَّب التَوصِيفِي}} are descriptive phrases. It consists of the \textarabic{مَوصُوف} (thing being described) and \textarabic{صِفَة} (descriptor). The rules can be remembered by the \textarabic{صفة} and \textarabic{موصوف} matching in \textbf{DING}:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{D}efiniteness
        \item \textbf{I}'raab
        \item \textbf{N}umber
        \item \textbf{G}ender
    \end{itemize}
\end{english}
